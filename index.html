Key road safety overview

Learning all the rules about being safe on the road before getting your prized drivers licence may seem a bit like a chore however, in reality this is a life or death situation. Ensuring you know all the rules and abide them at all times guarantees you a much closer path to success when it comes to Don't DIE: do not distract yourself, do not increase your speed over the speed limit, and do not engage in alcohol or drugs

Distractions

Distractions are essential to keep away from new drivers, and the improper use of a mobile phone alone makes up for over 1 in 5 crashes in Australia.


Learn More

Increased Speed

Increasing the speed of your driving might seem like a thrill, but in fact is essentially just one step closer to having a serious accident. It is estimated that 440 people die as a result of speeding, every single year.


Learn More

Engaging in illegal or intoxicating substances

Abusing any kind of intoxicating substance, whether alcohol or illicit drugs, is illegal for all new drivers and poses an enormous risk to your life and those around you if you are under their influence. They have the capability to make you drowsy and lose focus; things you do not want while driving.
